EDEM "THE MAGNIFICENT"

Edem Francois was born in Ghana, West Africa. Really beautiful place and this is why Edem has a very wonderful personality. Eventhough he was born and raised in Ghana, Edem is very open to expanding his horizon. After highschool, he moved to Canada to do his undergrad. Canada plays a very vital role in Edem's life. This is where he got his first job, met his first girlfriend, he joined the Army and so on. After 10 years in Canada, Edem had enough of Canada so moved to Madrid to pursue bigger dreams.

Early Life

Edem was born in Accra, the Capital of Ghana to really awesome parents.
He has 3 siblings and is the eldest of them. His main hobby was Basketball. He dreamt of becoming a basketball player, but God had bigger plans for him. (The story is still being told).

Education

Edem had all his elementary and highschool education in Ghana. In 2009, he moved to Canada to do his undergrad in Business Administration and graduated in 2015. He should have majored in Computer Science. The real world thought him that there are no interesting opportunities in the Business field. Everything was moving to Technology and this was very intriguing to him.

In September 2018, he enrolled in a Computer Science Master at IE University in Madrid. This will serve as a very defining moment in Edem's life and for his career. Edem met wonderful colleagues and made important connections such as Pepe Garcia. In July 2019, Edem graduated from IE and landed a job with Microsoft.

Professional Career

Edem's first real job after University was in Banking. In 2016, he started with CIBC bank. This bank job showed him exactly what he should not pursue in life; which is a career in Finance.

Edem also enlisted in the Canadian Armed Forces in 2017 as Signals Officer.

After his masters program in 2019, he joined Microsoft as a Data Scientist.
